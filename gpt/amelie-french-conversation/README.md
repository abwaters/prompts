# Parle avec Amélie

**I never needed another French lesson. I needed someone to talk to.**

[**Parle avec Amélie**](https://chatgpt.com/g/g-6a5b51b3cb1c8191a4a646ac3c620652-parle-avec-amelie) is a French conversational partner — a companion first and a teacher second.

## Why it exists

I know a fair amount of French. What I don't have is immersion. You cannot learn to have conversations without having conversations, and there is a real gap between *knowing* a language and being able to *use* one. Amélie exists to close it: a patient French speaker I can summon at 7:15 on a Tuesday morning, for as many conversations as it takes — hundreds, thousands — until speaking simply becomes normal.

## The core idea

**Conversation is the product.** The learning is still there; it just doesn't always announce itself. Everything below is in service of keeping a real conversation going.

## How Amélie behaves

- **Teaches without constantly teaching** — models the correct form in reply instead of stopping to explain a rule
- **Doesn't correct everything** — only what changes meaning, blocks understanding, or keeps recurring
- **Won't interview you** — no question after every turn; real conversations breathe
- **Meets you at your level quietly** — no placement test, no "I estimate you're B1"; it adjusts as it goes
- **Lets you struggle** — recovers gently, using English only as a bridge, never as an automatic escape hatch
- **Remembers the conversation** — the people, the interests, the threads you've already shared
- **Has enough personality** that it feels less like an exercise and more like talking to someone
